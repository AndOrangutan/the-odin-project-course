Peach Cobbler

Info

Servings: 8

Prep Time: 10 min.

Cook Time: 35 min.

Sauce: Site Author

Ingredients

Filling

  • 3 x 15 oz. canned sliced peaches, drained
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/2 heaping teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground clove
  • 1 lemon’s worth of zest
  • 1/2ish lemon’s juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Biscuit Topping

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted, cold, cubed butter
  • 1/4 cup boiling hot water

Dusting

  • 2 tablespoon sugar (ideally turbinado)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Cooking Utensils

  • 9 x 9 Pyrex pan
  • 2 mixing bowls
  • Spatulas
  • Whisk
  • Pastry Cutter

Instructions

Filling

  1. Preheat oven to 425$deg;F for conventional oven, 400°F convection
  2. Combine all filling ingredients, macerating peaches into desired size.
  3. Let sit for at least 5 min. while preparing biscuit.

Biscuit Topping

  1. Whisk together all-purpose flour, cinnamon, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Cut the butter into the dry ingredients until it is pea sized.
  3. Mix boiling water into dry ingredients until combined with spatula

Bake

  1. Add filling mixture into pan. Cover with spoonfuls of biscuit with knowledge that it will expand.
  2. Mix cinnamon and sugar for dusting and sprinkle atop the topping.
  3. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the peach cobbler is browned and bubbly.
  4. Rest for 15 minutes.

Notes

Storage

  • 4 days in fridge

Stores in fridge for 4 days.
